Subject: TG20/W251 — Emissions Control System (SCR + CO Catalyst) — Request for Budgetary Pricing

Dear CECO Environmental — Power Generation Solutions,

Trans World Power LLC is the program manager for a 50MW W251B8 natural gas power island
being developed for Borderplex in Santa Teresa, New Mexico (Project TG20/W251).

We are soliciting budgetary pricing for the Emissions Control package.

SCOPE OF SUPPLY:
- Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) system — NOx reduction to permit limits
- CO Catalyst system — CO oxidation
- Ammonia injection grid (AIG), ammonia storage and vaporization system
- Interconnecting ductwork, expansion joints, and structural support
- All instrumentation, controls integration, and DCS tie-in
- Commissioning support and performance testing

PROGRAM DETAILS:
- Site: Santa Teresa, NM (elevation ~4,100 ft, high desert environment)
- Fuel: Natural gas primary, fuel oil backup
- GT: Westinghouse W251B8, 50MW class, EthosEnergy Italia
- Standards: EPA MACT, New Mexico Environment Department permit limits
- Permit basis: NM AQMD permit (limits TBD — to be confirmed with response)

NOTE ON PERMIT LIMITS:
The NM Air Quality permit scope is being finalized. Budgetary pricing may be
based on typical 40–60MW natural gas GT installations. We will provide confirmed
permit limits prior to final scope and PO award.

Typical basis for budgetary purposes:
- NOx: ≤ 9 ppm @ 15% O2 (or best achievable technology)
- CO:  ≤ 10 ppm @ 15% O2
- PM:  per NM AQMD requirements

REQUEST:
1. Budgetary price (±20%) based on typical 50MW GT SCR+CO system
2. Lead time from PO to delivery at Santa Teresa NM
3. Clarification if price is significantly affected by permit limits
4. Reference list — similar 40–60MW GT installations (last 5 years)
5. Dimensional and weight data for civil/structural design

Note: We are issuing this RFQ to CECO Environmental and Peerless Manufacturing
simultaneously. Award based on price, technical merit, lead time, and references.
