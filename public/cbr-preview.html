I.

The studio behind the ledger.

Pl. i — Studio

CBR Trade Ltd was founded by Caren Raphael — a working artist whose second practice has, over twenty-five years, become one of the more quietly consequential trade-finance operations in private hands. The same instincts that govern her studio — composition, restraint, an eye for what others overlook — govern her approach to letters of credit, purchase-order finance, and the choreography of international commerce.

The firm structures capital for importers, exporters, manufacturers, distributors, and sovereign clients across four continents. It has moved gold and diamonds, gas turbines and rice, personal protective equipment in the worst weeks of a pandemic and clean water into regions that had none. Each engagement is approached the way an artist approaches a canvas: deliberately, with intention, and with attention to the whole composition.
